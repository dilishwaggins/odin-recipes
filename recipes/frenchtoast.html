FRENCH TOAST THE OJIJO WAY

Description

This is a meal, well technically its breakfast but my ex-boyfriend and I took it all any time. I'd like to think we even bonded over it.He'd bring it to my house when i wasnt feeling good.I'd go to his house and we'd have picnics where would eat it with coke and i remember those being the happiest moments of my life. He made it for me the first time and i remember thinking "Hmm i'm going to marry this man".It was like i'd never eaten french toast before. Every now and then i try and remake it in my own house using everything he used but i can never seem to get it right.Only he can make such an ordinary meal taste like a delicacy. I hope french toast makes people feel this same warmth.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon of pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • 4 slices of bread

Steps

  1. Whisk milk,eggs,vannilla,cinnamon together in a shallow bowl
  2. Lightly butter a pan and heat over medium-high heat
  3. Dunk bread in the egg mixture,soaking both sides.Transfer to the pan and cook until golden ,3 to 4 minutes per sude.
  4. Serve it hit with butter,maple syrup, chocolate syrup ,and/or fresh berries
